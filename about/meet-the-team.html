MEET THE TEAM

The talented individuals driving NMR Automotive's success

Seth Weaver

Owner

Seth began working on cars at 16 and went on to study automotive repair and performance. After six years in the industry and running his own detailing business, he founded NMR Automotive in 2019, moving into the shop's current location in 2020. Outside the shop, Seth enjoys working on his personal vehicles, spending time outdoors with his wife, Alaina, and being with friends and family. His goal is to continue growing NMR Automotive into a shop where customers and employees alike feel comfortable, confident, and valued.

Blake White

Technician

Blake joined the NMR Automotive team in May of 2025. He has been in the automotive industry for several years and is continuously expanding his skill set while earning additional certifications. Outside of the shop, Blake enjoys drifting and working on his drift car, drifting on the simulator, and spending time with his wife.
