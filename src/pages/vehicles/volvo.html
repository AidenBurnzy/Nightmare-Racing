Services we handle

Whether you daily your Volvo or push it hard on weekends, we keep it reliable and responsive.

Maintenance & reliability

Factory-interval maintenance with a performance eye.

  • Oil, fluid, and filter services

Diagnostics & drivability

Root-cause testing with proof in data and photos.

  • Check engine, misfire, and drivability issues
  • Noise, vibration, suspension, and steering
  • Fuel system, air/fuel trims, boost leaks
  • HVAC performance and electrical gremlins

Performance & setup

Upgrades tuned for real-world reliability.

  • Coilovers, springs, sway bars, and alignments
  • Exhaust fabrication, intakes, and intercooler plumbing
  • Tuning support prep, plugs, coils, injectors, pumps
  • Track-day inspections and fastener checks

Fabrication & custom

Purpose-built metal work to match your goals.

  • Custom exhaust sections and repairs
  • Brackets, tabs, and small chassis solutions
  • Intake, charge pipe, and catch can routing
  • Clean wiring repairs and loom tidy-ups

Common visits for Volvo

Comfort & electrical

  • Lighting, sensors, and wiring repairs
  • Battery, alternator, and starter testing
